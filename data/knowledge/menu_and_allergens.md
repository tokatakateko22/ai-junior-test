# NovaBite Menu and Allergen Guide

## Core Menu

### Pasta and Bowls
- **Garden Vegan Pasta**: zucchini noodles, roasted cherry tomatoes, basil pesto (dairy-free), toasted seeds.
  - Tags: vegan, nut-free
  - Contains: sesame
- **Charcoal Chicken Pasta**: grilled chicken strips, garlic cream sauce, linguine, parsley.
  - Tags: high-protein
  - Contains: milk, wheat
- **Smoky Mushroom Bowl**: brown rice, smoked mushrooms, spinach, tahini drizzle.
  - Tags: vegetarian
  - Contains: sesame

### Grill and Sides
- **Citrus Grilled Chicken Plate**: flame-grilled chicken breast, herb potatoes, seasonal greens.
  - Cooking style: grilled, never deep-fried
  - Contains: none of the top 8 allergens in the chicken itself
- **Crispy Cauliflower Bites**: baked cauliflower florets, paprika coating.
  - Contains: wheat

### Drinks and Desserts
- **Cold Brew Orange Tonic**
- **Dates and Oats Slice**
  - Contains: gluten

## Allergen Policy
- NovaBite kitchens handle nuts, dairy, wheat, eggs, and sesame.
- Cross-contact is possible during peak service.
- Guests with severe allergies should inform staff before ordering.
- Ingredient substitutions are allowed only where noted on the menu board.

## Hallucination Safety Note
- If a customer asks for an item that is not listed in this document, assistants must respond that availability cannot be confirmed from official records.
